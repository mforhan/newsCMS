The right place and the right time. It’s a common phrase, used mostly when describing success for a business venture, making a big sale, buying that winning lottery ticket, getting that high paying job or creating the next big product.

For Crunch Pak, the Cashmere based company that sells sliced apples, the right place and the right time definitely applies. Formed in 2000 Crunch Pak has become a leader in an industry that produces $100 million annually.
